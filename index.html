About digital sustainability

Climate change is one of the greatest problems we are facing. According to the World Health Organisation (WHO), climate change is expected to cause an additional 250,000 deaths annually between 2030 and 2050.

Digital technology has great potential to help in the fight against climate change, but more needs to be done to ensure that it does not exacerbate the problem further. Currently, the internet alone accounts for approximately 3.8% of global carbon emissions. This is where awareness of digital sustainability comes in.

Gerry McGovern is a leading voice in the digital sustainability and e-waste space. He uses his platform to educate and spread information about the current state of the digital landscape in relation to climate change.
